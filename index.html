IT Technologies.

Clouds, services, servers

What does it do?

What is the state of the art of this new technology?
At the moment, there are many forms and uses of clouds, services and servers. One that is well known is Google Drive,  which is an online storage service that allows a user to not only store their files, but to also share it or even download files from other users. Other forms of clouds, services and servers are used by organisations to store their data and information

What can be done now?
As of right now, clouds, services and servers provide many benefits to society. Currently, we are able to store files, data and information online that can be accessed at any location as long as the user has access to the internet. This is extremely helpful for businesses and individuals who are always on the move and require large amounts of storage without having to carry around a portable hard drive, USB, etc.  Clouds, services and servers also allows individual to share their work with others, regardless of where their location is, through the internet mass amounts of data, information and files could be sent to anywhere on the globe. We can see this almost everywhere, one example being, when a customer purchases music, movies, games, etc. online and they download it, such files are stored on a server where it can be easily downloaded by countless amount of customers. Not only does this pose as a benefit to the customer, but also to the business/company as they can expand the sales they make not only through physical copies but also through digital. Overall this allows the transactions between business and customers to be almost effortless as they don’t need to physically go to shops anymore to purchase these items, this would also encourage the customer to keep using this platform to purchase their needs and wants. Another example highlighting the benefits of clouds, services and servers is the way that companies and businesses use them in order to backup their information and data and to also share it with their employees. While business/companies still use onsite physical servers to store their data and information, many are opting to use cloud services to store their data and information. Due to the fact that this decreases the risk from environmental threats, accidental threats and manmade threats. However, the company/business would be allowing a third party access to their data and information so the use of services, clouds and servers also come with its cons.

What is likely to be able to do be done soon (say in the next 3 years)?
At the current rate of progression in technological advances, in the next 3 years it is likely that the storage that clouds, services and servers provide would continue to increase. As of right now gigabytes to terabytes to zettabytes of storage are available for companies to store their data and information on. In the current future it is possible that the these amounts could increase, that would allow for a massive boost in data and information stored on these clouds, services and servers.  Another possibility is that the speed of transfer, whether it be uploading or downloading, would increase in the future. As of right now this speed is highly dependant on the speed of the internet being used by the individual/company/business. As our technology advances it is possible that our internet speeds would receive an increase in speed which of course would reflect on the speed of transfer provided by these cloud servers and services.

What technological or other developments make this possible?
The factor that would allow for these improvements on clouds, services and servers is the progression in our understanding of technology. As the years go by and the more we evolve with our technology, the more possibilities that we can create in regards to clouds, services and servers. By also researching and developing better understanding of digital storage and the internet, we could improve the state of our current cloud, servers and services.

What is the likely impact?

Which people will be most affected and how?
It is hard to determine which target group would be the most impacted as both companies/businesses and individuals use clouds, servers and services. It would be best to say that both parties would be affected positively, with the progressions being made. On the business/company side of things, they would be affected as improvements on clouds, services and servers could improve the way that the business/company functions. It could increase the efficiency and effectiveness of how they perform tasks, for example an improvement of speed and storage space would allow a company to perform faster backups, if they were originally undergoing weekly backups for example because of the improvement on the speed and storage, they could upgrade into doing daily full backups so that they could improve the effectiveness of their data storing procedures. On the other hand, individuals such as customers or employees, would be impacted positively by the increase in speed and storage of the cloud, services and servers, due to the fact that they would be able to download files faster whether it be a file related to work or something such as a movie, game or album. Uploading data, information and files would also benefit, since the storage space and speed increases, the user is given the opportunity to store more items through cloud.

Will this create, replace or make redundant any current jobs or technologies?
As our internet and storage facilities upgrade their current services and functions, technology such as USBs and portable hard drives could be made obsolete by the improvements on clouds, servers and services. Due to the fact that these days, most places have access to internet and thus the process of storing data on the internet could prove to be a more effective and efficient way of storing data on the go.

In your daily life, how will this affect you?
The use of clouds, services and servers affects my daily life quite heavily due to the fact that I am a university student and most of the work that I do is stored on Google Drive. This platform of storing is crucial in my daily life as not only does it allow me to store my work on it, but it also gives me the ability to collaborate or share my work with other students or people.

What will be different for you?
If in the current future, the speed and the storage capacity of clouds, services and servers are improved it could mean a huge difference to me. This is because I am an individual that likes to store my work, files, data and information online so that I can access it on the go. If the capacity of clouds services increase, it is possible that I might not even need to use the storage that my laptop provides but instead store everything online as it would achieve the same thing and also provides more benefits. Also, since I am an individual that likes to download things such as games, music and movies though the internet, if there was an increase in the speed of transfer in regards to cloud, services and servers. I would be more inclined in purchasing my needs and wants online as it would be more effective and efficient than purchasing it physically.

Blockchain and Cryptocurrency

What does it do?

What is the state of the art of this new technology?
To begin, Cryptocurrency is a type of currency that does not take the usual physical form of currency. It resides online and is not supported by the government but instead supported by the networks that are responsible for them. As of right now there are many forms of cryptocurrency ranging from Bitcoin, Dash, Ethereum, Litecoin and many more. Along with this, they also vary in the value that they possess in the market, with Bitcoin leading the charge with a current value of over $5000 for one individual Bitcoin. The prices of Cryptocurrencies are not set in stone as many factors will be taken into account that will determine the value that it possesses, they could range from a couple of cents to a couple of thousands in dollars. This type of currency is stored in a digital wallet that could be accessed through a desktop or on a phone, this wallet allows the customer to perform secure transactions to send, receive, sell or buy all forms of digital currency.

What can be done now?
As of right now, there are stores and companies that accept some forms of cryptocurrency, most often Bitcoin, when a customer decides to use this form of payment. However due to its nature, many stores and companies choose not to deal in Cryptocurrency or allow the option to do so, due to the fact that the value that it possesses is not set and could fluctuate at any time. There are already a huge number of things that you could purchase through cryptocurrency, whether it be digitally or physically. Things such as games, cars, giftcards, clothes, flights and even hotels are able to be purchased or used through the transaction of cryptocurrency but the difficulty in doing so would be finding the seller or company that would allow for such transaction to occur in the first place.

What is likely to be able to do be done soon (say in the next 3 years)?
In the next 3 years, it is possible that more companies and stores will accept the form of cryptocurrency as a payment. Meaning that it could be a regular thing in the future for an individual to use something like Litecoin, which has less value than Bitcoin but is more affordable, to purchase their needs or wants whether it be online or physically.  Another possibility is that there are more types of cryptocurrencies that will be released in the future that also range in their value on the market, along with this is the possibility that the value of current cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, could double its price or more surprisingly, suffer a huge loss in its value.

 

What technological or other developments make this possible?
The developments that will allow for these possibilities to occur is the evolution of our society and the way that we are trying to discover new ways of doing things. As the years go by, there is a good possibility that applications will be created that will allow companies or stores to deal in cryptocurrency. Which could result in a boost of transactions being completed through cryptocurrency and could rival that of the normal currency we have at the moment.

Project Ideas.

Smart Wardrobe: Robodrobe

The RoboDrobe is smart and automated wardrobe. The RoboDrobe is a premium product and as such will provide a world class user experience. Initially, the wardrobe will be tailored in size and capacity according to the users’ needs for the ultimate user experience. The wardrobe will be equipped with voice recognition and a large mirror that also functions as a touch screen panel that displays and controls all the parts of the smart wardrobe, allowing for the user to set up a multitude of preferences that will allow the wardrobe to function on its own overtime. The panel will also display features such as forecasts, current fashion trends, suggest pieces of clothing that the user can purchase to complete  fashionable outfits as well as the time in the top corner of the display. The wardrobe will be linked to the internet as well as the users’ calendar, allowing for the wardrobe to be aware of the temperature and the location/dress-code of the event you will be attending. The wardrobe will have an input on the rightmost closet that accepts any clothing that the user places into it and sorts that clothing out into categories for both the wardrobe and the user to utilise. Integration with the users social media and email will allow them to receive notifications regarding current trends and sales from their favourite brands. The user can setup and name his/her own presets or allow the wardrobe to make its own presets that the user can view and modify via the touch screen panel. The user will be given three main options: manually choosing pieces of clothing and hence using the smart wardrobe as a regular one whenever he/she wishes, allowing the smart wardrobe to come up with an outfit based on the time of the event and the occasion based off online trends and fashion databases through utilisation of present clothing that the user inputs into the system, and finally the user can create a set of presets for their favourite outfits that they can retrieve  on demand for maximum convenience. Those are the primary features of the wardrobe, however for extra costs the wardrobe can be fitted with attachments to further make the users’ experience truly unique. An example of an attachment the user could purchase is an ironing station that can be attached to the clothing input which automatically irons and handles any clothing present using robotic arms that detect and manoeuvre the clothing appropriately and carefully before placing said clothing into the wardrobe input for sorting. Although not specified above, the smart wardrobe caters for all pieces of clothing, not just torso and leg clothing. This ranges from: hats, gloves, shoes, scarves, ties etc. All of these features merge seamlessly together on the glass display to allow for the best possible user experience a wardrobe can provide on the market.
